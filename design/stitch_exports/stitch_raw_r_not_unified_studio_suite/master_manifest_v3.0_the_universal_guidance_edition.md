## 2. Universal Guidance: The "Ampliación" Framework (NEW)
To ensure universal accessibility and clarity across all device paradigms (Vision Pro, Desktop, Mobile, Watch), the platform implements a dual-tier guidance system:

### A. Ampliación Automática (Intelligent Context)
- **Mechanism:** Every interactive UI element (buttons, cards, toggles) triggers an explanatory overlay on **hover, gaze-focus (XR), or proximity (Apple Pencil)**.
- **Content:** Briefly explains *What* it is, *Why* it exists, and *How* to use it.
- **Implementation:** CSS/SwiftUI-native tooltips with zero-latency triggers.

### B. Ampliación Extra (Deep Context)
- **Mechanism:** Small, high-contrast interactive icons (?, !, ⚠, ⓘ) placed strategically next to complex features.
- **Content:** Provides deep operational manuals, legal precautions, technical specs, and creative commentary.
- **UI Aesthetic:** Matches the active theme (Glassmorphic for Obsidian, Ink-stamped for Lightcraft).

## 3. Aesthetic Governance: Obsidian vs. Lightcraft
- **Obsidian Dark:** Futuristic, high-contrast glassmorphism.
