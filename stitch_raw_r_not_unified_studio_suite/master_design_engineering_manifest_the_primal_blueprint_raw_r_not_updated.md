# Master Design & Engineering Manifest: The Primal Blueprint (raw’r’not) - Updated

## 1. Executive Summary & Vision
**Brand:** raw’r’not
**North Star:** "The Digital Apex" — A high-fidelity, Apple-native ecosystem blending "Super-Futuristic" precision with "Craft-y-Arte" soul.
**Core Objective:** Capture user attention through behavioral economics (nudges, gamification) and cinematic storytelling, replacing social media consumption with a high-value transactive "Single-Player" experience.

## 2. Technical Architecture (MACH & Native)
Based on the deep research provided in {{DATA:DOCUMENT:DOCUMENT_6}}, {{DATA:DOCUMENT:DOCUMENT_9}}, and {{DATA:DOCUMENT:DOCUMENT_10}}.

### A. Backend: Composable & Headless
- **Architecture:** MACH (Microservices, API-first, Cloud-native, Headless).
- **Hosting:** Google Cloud Platform (Cloud Run for compute, Cloud SQL for relational data, Memorystore for Redis for low-latency caching).
- **IA Integration:** Model Context Protocol (MCP) server for standardized agentic interaction with CRM, CMS, and transactional logic.
- **Multitenancy:** Metadata-driven architecture with Row-Level Security (RLS) to ensure data isolation.

### B. Frontend: Omnichannel & Apple-Native
- **Web App:** Built with Next.js (App Router), leveraging a shared design system. Fully compliant with Apple's visual standards (Liquid Glass).
- **Native Ecosystem:** Swift/SwiftUI for iOS, macOS, iPadOS, and visionOS.
- **Persistence:** Offline-first strategy using SwiftData/Core Data for seamless "Flow" state.

## 3. Design System: Obsidian Roar
See details in {{DATA:DESIGN_SYSTEM:DESIGN_SYSTEM_1}} and {{DATA:DOCUMENT:DOCUMENT_7}}.

- **Aesthetic:** High-contrast Dark Mode, Newsreader typography (Newsreader + Inter Mono), and "Liquid Glass" materials.

- **Color Palette:** Archival tones (Indigos, Charcoals, Sepias, Newspaper Off-White). Prohibited: #000000.
- **Motion:** Mechanical, linear transitions (camera shutter, flip-clock) instead of fluid bounces.

## 4. Behavioral & Gamification Strategy
Based on {{DATA:DOCUMENT:DOCUMENT_8}}.

- **The Hook:** "Single-Player Mode" gamification (Dominance Meters, Creative Streaks, Lion Heart quality meters).
- **Nudges:** 
    - **Anchoring:** Displaying original/historical pricing to frame value.
    - **Loss Aversion:** Framing rewards as "don't lose your discount."
    - **Zeigarnik Effect:** Persistent progress bars for unfinished tasks (briefs, profiles).
- **Viral Engine:** Externalized Group-Forming Network (Reed's Law) — encouraging "Team Buys" via external messaging (WhatsApp/iMessage) to keep the platform social-noise-free.

## 5. Financial & Transactional Logic (New)
See {{DATA:DOCUMENT:DOCUMENT_31}} for market research.

- **Primary Payment:** SINPE Móvil & SINPE IBAN (Costa Rica). Prioritizes merchant-initiated "Request for Payment" to eliminate verification lag.
- **Proof of Transfer:** Manual upload of bank vouchers supported as a legacy/small-business fallback, integrated with OCR for automated verification.
- **Internal Currency:** "rawrs" tokens. Purchased in tiered packages (Cub, Lion, Apex) to facilitate frictionless micro-transactions within the ecosystem.
- **Shadow Profiles:** Guest data (emails, specs) is captured into "Shadow Accounts" using Progressive Profiling, incentivizing registration through the "Claim your Profile" nudge post-transaction.
